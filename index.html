Welcome to the World — One Planet, Endless Stories

The world is home to over 195 countries, each with its own history, culture, language, and system of governance. These countries are spread across seven continents — Asia, Africa, North America, South America, Antarctica, Europe, and Australia.

Countries & Nations

A country is a defined geographical area with recognized borders and sovereignty.

A nation refers more to the people — often bound by shared identity, culture, or ethnicity. Some nations exist within countries, while some countries contain multiple nations.

Some nations dream of becoming countries. It’s complex — and fascinating.

Diverse Systems

  • Government types (e.g., democracy, monarchy, republic)
  • Laws & legal systems
  • Religions & belief systems
  • Languages (there are over 7,000 languages worldwide!)
  • Traditions and customs unique to their history

Global Challenges

  • Climate change
  • Economic inequality
  • Migration
  • Peace & conflict
  • Technological shifts

Unity in Diversity

Organizations like the United Nations promote peace, cooperation, and development among countries, reminding us that we share one planet and a common future.
