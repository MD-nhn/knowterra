Welcome to the World — One Planet, Endless Stories

The world is home to over 195 countries, each with its own history, culture, language, and way of life. These countries span across seven continents — Asia, Africa, North America, South America, Antarctica, Europe, and Australia — each carrying stories as old as time.

Countries & Nations

A country is a defined land with borders, sovereignty, and often, a flag and anthem.

A nation, however, is about the people — connected by culture, language, or ethnic roots.

Some nations live within countries. Some stretch across them. And some… are still seeking a place to call their own.
